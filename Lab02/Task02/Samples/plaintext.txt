In cryptography we often assume the attacker is clever patient and lucky. We do not assume the attacker is lazy. This plain text is meant to simulate an English message that could in theory have been intercepted on a noisy line or copied by hand with errors, spacing issues, and punctuation noise. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog, but the lazy dog was not impressed and decided to file a formal complaint about excessive jumping near residential areas during night hours.
